# Brain-Tumor-Analysis
Analysis of Brain Tumor Disease  Analysis using PowerBi
# Overview
This Analysis shows the differnts types of Brain Tumor Diseases stating the top most affected parts of the Brain and most affected Age Groups. According to the Analysis, people from age 60+ suffers Brain Tumor the most.
# Key Insight
. Brain Tumor Diseases affects all age group but the most affected age group is from 60+ upwards
. The Analysis shows the differnts types of Brain Tumor Diseases
. Year with highest diagnoses of Brain Tumor Diseases
. Most affected Brain Region
# Tools
. PowerBi
. DAX for KPIs Measures
. Power Query to clean Data
. PowerPoints to build DashBoard Template
