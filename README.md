# Brain-Tumor-Analysis
Analysis of Brain Tumor Disease  Analysis using PowerBi
# Overview
This Analysis shows the differnts types of Brain Tumor Diseases stating the top most affected parts of the Brain and most affected Age Groups. This Brain Tumor Analysis project focused on exploring and analyzing Brain Tumor related data to identify patterns,trends and insights that can support research and data driven decision-making. the project involves cleaning, analyzing and visualizing medical data to better understand tumor types, patients classification and key variables associated with Brain Tumor.

The analyis emphasizes data interpretation and visualization and also demonstrate the use of analytical tools to extract meaningful insights from complex healthcare datasets.

# Key Insight
. Brain Tumor Diseases affects all age group but the most affected age group is from 60+ upwards
. The Analysis shows the differnts types of Brain Tumor Diseases
. Year with highest diagnoses of Brain Tumor Diseases
. Most affected Brain Region
